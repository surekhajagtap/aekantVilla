About the Villa

A Peaceful Retreat by the Vaitarna Backwaters

Nestled amidst the serene beauty of the Vaitarna Dam backwaters, Aekant Villa is a peaceful retreat designed for those seeking relaxation, comfort, and unforgettable moments with family and friends.

Wake up to mesmerizing lake views, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and immerse yourself in nature's tranquility. Whether it's a weekend getaway, a romantic escape, a family vacation, or a celebration with loved ones, Aekant Villa offers the perfect blend of luxury and solitude.
